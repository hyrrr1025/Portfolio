DEJA VU
BRAND DESIGN
In French, Deja vu means 'already seen'â€”that uncanny sense of familiarity. This connects deeply with the Proust Effect, which reveals how scent has the unique power to transport us back in time, unlocking specific memories from our past.
The Deja vu brand is dedicated to using fragrance to transport you back to the moments you most long to revisit. Our logo features a ribbon design, with a dreamy color palette and flowing lines that mirror the nature of scent itself. Though ephemeral and intangible, like the faintest of clues, this scent possesses the strength to hold up the entire edifice of memory.
Design Vision
The packaging for 'Deja vu' features a butterfly motif, a symbol of beauty and transformation. Although humans are social beings, we often must face life's hardest moments in solitude. It is our hope that this fragrance helps you rediscover your most cherished memories, and that those memories sustain you, becoming the courage you need to step into the future.
